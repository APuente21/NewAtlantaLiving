Silver Comet Trail

Saturday, July 15, 2017

One of the things I miss the most about New York City is central park. Every weekend I would wake up early to go jogging along the big loop, which is about 6 miles long. The trail is for the most part surrounded by tall trees which hide most of the buildings from sight; it is one of the few places in NYC that you can go to escape the hustle and bustle of the city and feel more connected to nature.

Our Move to Atlanta

Tuesday, March 14, 2017

Or being stucked in the subway for over forty minutes because a car had somehow been swept onto the train tracks? Doesn't everyone like paying $3k, a month, for a 500 sqr ft apartment on a forth floor walk up with no amenities? Needless to say, I was pretty darn static when the day of the move finally arrived.
